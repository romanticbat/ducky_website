🌼 What does it do?

♡ Ducky is an adorable bot created so that your players can have an immersive experience between trainer and their Pokémon. Care for an adorable virtual pokémon, showering it with love, food, and virtual attention.

♡ Feed, pet, and play with your pokémon to keep it happy and healthy. See pokémon evolve based on interactions and achievements !

♡ Unlock new activities to interact with your pokémon and your friends' pokémon. All of this with a cute and adorable interface to see all the information about your Pokémon's current state!

🌱 Ducky Commands

Main Commands

/start — Begin your Pokémon journey!

/profile — View your trainer profile.

/inventory — Check your items in your bag.

Interaction Commands

/feed — Give food to your Pokémon.

/play — Play with your Pokémon to increase happiness.

/bath — Give your Pokémon a relaxing bath.

/train — Train and improve your Pokémon’s stats.

/pokémon — View your Pokémon’s details.

Battle & Adventure

/battle — Challenge another trainer!

/quests — Complete fun tasks.

/daily — Claim your daily reward.

/cooldowns — Check your current cooldowns.

/leaderboard — See who’s the best trainer!

Trainer Progress

/learn — Teach new moves to your Pokémon.

/evolve — Evolve your Pokémon.

/held — Give an item to your partner.

Shop & Extras

/shop — Browse the item shop.

/buy — Purchase items from the shop.

/vote — Support Ducky Bot and earn rewards!
